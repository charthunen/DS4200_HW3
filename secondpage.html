Hiking

My love for hiking comes from growing up in Washington, but I try to explore new trails wherever I go! This past year, I went on hikes in three different states and Puerto Rico:

  • Virginia: A 9.3-mile hike in Shenandoah National Park.
  • Washington: Favorite trails include Mason Lake, Annette Lake, and Rattlesnake Ledge.
  • New Hampshire: Hiked Mount Major in the fall for incredible foliage views.
  • Puerto Rico: Explored El Yunque National Forest and did a waterfall hike.

I'm hoping 2025 will be a year with even more incredible hikes!

Reading

My reading habits tend to cycle—I can go from reading a book a day when I have free time to not reading one for a month when I'm busy. When I do read, I enjoy Thrillers, Historical Fiction, and Fantasy. According to my Goodreads "My Year in Books," I read 25 books in 2024. Some of my highlights:

  • A Court of Thorns and Roses by Sarah J. Maas (5-book series)
  • The Silent Patient by Alex Michaelides
  • Throne of Glass by Sarah J. Maas (still in progress)

My goal for 2025 is 30 books— which I am curently behind on, so I need to catch up!

Food

I went vegetarian in 2020 and have enjoyed exploring Boston’s veggie-friendly restaurants:

  • Krasi: Mediterranean food in Back Bay—my all-time favorite!
  • Carmelina’s: North End Italian spot worth the visit.
  • Banyan Bar + Refuge: Great Asian Fusion with veg options.
  • Mela: Best Indian food in the South End!
